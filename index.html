[ What We Offer ]

Full-Service Detailing

From a quick refresh to a full restoration — every service you need to keep your vehicle in showroom condition.

✨

Full Exterior Detail

Hand wash, clay bar treatment, decontamination, polish, and a deep-gloss finish that turns heads on the road.

🪑

Full Interior Detail

Vacuuming, steam cleaning, stain removal, and conditioning of every surface — leaving your cabin spotless and fresh.

⚙️

Engine Bay Cleaning

Safe degreasing and detailing of your engine bay — removing grime, oil buildup, and leaving it factory-new.

💡

Headlight Polishing

Restore cloudy, yellowed headlights to crystal clarity — improving both the look and safety of your vehicle.

🪵

Leather Restoration

Deep clean, condition, and restore leather seats and trim — preventing cracking and bringing back that rich feel.

🔬

Paint Correction

Multi-stage machine polishing to eliminate swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation — restoring your paint's true depth.

🛡️

Paint Protection

Ceramic coatings, paint sealants, and wax protection to guard your finish against the harsh Calgary elements.

🚿

Odor Elimination

Ozone treatment and deep interior sanitization to completely eliminate stubborn odors from pets, smoke, or spills.

🌟

And Much More

Every detail matters. If you don't see your service listed, reach out — if it involves detailing, we likely do it.
